OoBiZu Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025
Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

OoBiZu ("we," "our," or "us") develops games and applications for multiple platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use any OoBiZu app, website, or online service (collectively, the "Services").

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Our games are built with a privacy‑first philosophy. We do not collect, store, or share:

  • • Personal identifiers such as name, email, or address
  • • Biometric information or device-specific identifiers
  • • Precise location or motion tracking data
  • • Camera, microphone, or sensor data

All such information remains solely on the device and under the platform's privacy protections.

3. Information We May Collect

For performance and quality assurance, we may collect limited non‑personal data such as:

  • • Anonymous gameplay analytics (e.g., session duration, feature usage)
  • • Aggregated crash or stability logs provided by the platform
  • • Device model, operating system version, and app build identifier

This data cannot identify you individually and is used exclusively for optimization.

4. Use of Information

  • • Improve gameplay performance and user experience
  • • Detect bugs and ensure software stability
  • • Guide feature development using anonymous engagement patterns

OoBiZu does not sell or share data for advertising or marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Retention

No user data is hosted by OoBiZu directly. Limited analytics provided by the platform follow their encrypted retention policy. Any temporary diagnostic data is purged after aggregate analysis.

6. Third‑Party Services

Where our apps leverage third-party platforms (app stores, cloud services, gaming services), data is processed under the respective platform's Privacy Policy. OoBiZu does not have access to your personal account information from these services.

7. Children's Privacy

OoBiZu's games are suitable for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 (16 where required by law). Parents may contact us to request support or deletion.

8. Data Security

Our applications rely on the device's built-in security features and on‑device processing for all sensitive data operations. No sensitive information leaves your device.

9. User Rights

Depending on your region, you may be entitled to request access to, deletion of, or information about anonymized data. Such requests can be made by contacting us directly via email.
